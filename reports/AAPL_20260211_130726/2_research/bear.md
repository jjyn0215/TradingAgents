You call Apple’s lower Capex "discipline." By avoiding the "Capex Trap," Apple is essentially outsourcing its future intelligence to the very competitors it seeks to disrupt. If on-device AI can’t match the reasoning power of the cloud-based giants, the iPhone becomes just a glorified terminal for Google or OpenAI’s services. In that scenario, Apple loses its most valuable asset: **the soul of the user experience.** Your "margin protection" argument ignores the fact that if Apple falls behind in AI capability, they lose the hardware premium altogether.
